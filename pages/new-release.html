Jeshas Keep

Sequel to The Cold Pact

Decan

Having just escaped with his life and the lives of Bay 6, Decan makes his way through the North country towards whatever Zo has only described as ‘tower’. With no other guide to go by, and with the constant threats of beasts, hunger, and begin discovered by soldiers, Declan leads his bay in a search for safety.

Secar

Reports continue to flood in from northern battalions regarding the attack on Illengra. Rumors of soldiers in glowing silver armour occupy Secar’s mind. With many of his forces wiped out or missing, he must grapple with how you fight a force that kills the very ground it walks on, and how you lead a kingdom when you’re mad?

Jacquelin and Allysin

Elsewhere, Jacquelin and Allysin travel with haste to reach Secar’s Assembly. With their envoy of soldiers, they can only hope to arrive before the king is killed by this new enemy, or by his own court.
